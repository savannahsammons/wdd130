Why should you learn to read sheet music? Reading sheet music allows you to pick up any written song and learn to play it regardless of your skill level. You may not be able to quite corrdinate your fingers to play like the song is supposed to sound right away, but with practice you will eventually get it down. If you do not know how to read sheet music you will be relying on muscle memory that fades fast with time. You need to still practice reading sheet music to remember it, however whatever you learn will last a lot longer and be more beneficial to you.
